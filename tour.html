Welcome to uSell Auctions

Our Auctions allow professional buyers like you to acquire used phone and tablet inventory that you can resell with confidence. With our easy to use platform you can buy the devices and quantities that work for you. All phones are fully tested and graded into specific conditions so you know exactly what you’re getting.

 

Here’s how it works...

1.  See What’s Available

The Auction page shows you all the Lots that are available. You can bid on all devices that interest you before the auction ends.

You can view a detailed condition report for every lot. We provide full transparency to help you make savvy buying decisions. All devices are graded using a standard scale

2. Place Your Bids

Available: Indicates the number of devices that are available within a Lot

 

Minimum Bid: Is the minimum price per unit you must offer for us to consider your bid

 

Buyout Price: Is the take all price for the entire Lot, to be offered on a first come first serve basis

Add Additional Bids

You can bid for additional quantity within the same lot at a lower per unit price

3. Win!

We’ll announce results of the Auction soon after it closes. Log back in to see details of your winnings.
